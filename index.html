Introduction

The dataset that we have chosen is the Happiness dataset on Kaggle.com from the World Happiness Report. Happiness score is a metric measured by asking the sampled population the question: â€œHow would you rate your happiness on a scale of 0 to 10, where 10 is the happiest. This dataset gives a Happiness score by country for 155 countries with factors that account for Happiness to include:

  • Economy (GDP per Capita)

  • Family

  • Health (Life Expectancy)

  • Freedom

  • Trust (Government Corruption)

  • Generosity

  • Dystopia Residual

    • Each of these metrics when added together gives a Happiness score between 1-10.

    Mission

    Determine if we can take data from other sources for 3 of the 7 factors of Happiness, while adding in fertility, and predict Happiness score for 2018 and have the user put in their own factors to predict their happiness score
